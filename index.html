    Amazing Features

    An easy to use video and audio redaction system powered by Artificial Intelligence (AI) and packed with features to make redaction experience simple. The semi-automated redaction process allows you to control the redaction process the way you want

    • Easy-to-use Redact your video with 3 simple steps, (1) Upload video file, (2) Redact video and (3) Download redacted video file
    • Redact Audio Choose audio segments from your video file and redact the audio using custom styles
    • Customizable Styles Choose your redaction styling from a selection of pixelate, mask, smooth and solid face blur stylings
    • Video Editing Use the simple video editing tool to simplify your video before redacting
    • Automatic Face Detection Our advanced visual analytics algorithms make sure all faces are detected accurately
    • Powered by AI The advanced Artificial Intelligence (AI) engine integrated in the Redaction System ensures all faces are detected and recognized in every angle and every position with 99% accuracy
